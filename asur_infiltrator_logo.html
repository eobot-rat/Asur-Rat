About the Logo

The Asur Infiltrator logo combines modern cybersecurity aesthetics with mythological Asura symbolism. The design features a central Asura-inspired emblem with the characteristic "third eye," representing enhanced knowledge and perception, surrounded by digital data streams and matrix elements.

The purple and cyan color scheme represents power and technology, while the hexagonal shield suggests security and protection. Orbiting data nodes symbolize the framework's comprehensive monitoring capabilities.
